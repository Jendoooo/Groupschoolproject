Entrance Examinations

Basic Education council examination The School’s Entrance Exam comprises 9 papers. Paper 1- English and Mathematics Paper 2- BST Paper 3 - RNV Paper 4- Yoruba and French Language Paper 5- PVS Paper 6- Business Studies Paper 7- Test into other classes apart from Year 7 is based on the class which the applicant is seeking admission into. Although the general entrance examinations are scheduled for between April and July, special entrance examination sessions are also scheduled for other dates depending on the availability of space in the applicant’s desired year group. It is important for applicants to bring along their writing materials, mathematical sets and a non-programmable calculator to sit the entrance examination.

To complete your child registration, all document below photocopies must be submitted to the school authority :

  1. 2 passport photograph of applicant not more than 2 years old
  2. applicant’s date of birth certificate
  3. applicant’s lassra
  4. parents/guardians tax clearance

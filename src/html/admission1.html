The Ogunmodede Senior College, renowned institution known for moulding future leaders take students under the directives of the Lagos Education District 3.

Admission into SS1 is usually under the prerogative of the district but we can provide advice to put your ward in the best position for admission.
However, is important to state that the child must meet the age requirement before the application form can be acquired by parents.

Admission Consideration

Admission into the Ogunmodede Senior College involves much more than considering academic abilities. We seek to ensure that the Ogunmodede Senior College is the perfect fit for every child and parent applying. Our goal is to see every new pupil grow and succeed both in and out of our school and we are particular about maintaining the right environment to ensure this.

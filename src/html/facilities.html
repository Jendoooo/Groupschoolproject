Ultra Modern ICT laboratory

As facilitated by the Member representing Epe Federal Constituency at the House of Representatives, Hon Wale Raji and commisisoned by the Speaker ,Federal House of Representatives, Rt Hon Femi Gbajabiamila, the school has a state of the art ICT - Laboratory with HP all in one touch screens computers.
The computers have Intel Pentium Silver processors with moderate processing power, HD webcams enabling our students to navigate the world of tech which birthed this website.

Comprehensive Classes

In line with keeping within touching distance, resource led growth of our dear state, the Lagos state government established six comprehensivve colleges with our school housing the only one in education district 3. In here we teach courses outside the regular ones students would sit in ordinary level exams.

24-7 Solar Powered Eletricity

During Governor Raji Fashola's adminstration , a reform of the educational sector continued with the installation of solar powered elecctricity in public secondary schools in Lagos . And with successive adminstration of our school under different heads, we have been able to maintain the facility such that it powers the school for as long as we needed.

Modern Science Laboratory

Our success in WASSCE and NECO Examinations is fuelled not only by the hardwork of our teachers but also the good state of our science laboratories. It is central to our ovewhelmingly positive performance in the stated exams.

ABOUT US

Over 40 years of existence our school has impact great knowledge on our student to make them sucessful in life

Founded in 1980, Ogunmodede Senior College, Epe has built students who have gone on to led the state and Nation in various sectors of our economy. Here, we produce pacesetters .

MISSION

To produce highly diciplined students with sound,academic and exra curricular abilities capable of making them secure addmision into tertiary institution with less stress through the creation of beautiful and conducive environment and with capacity to influence environment and with capacity to influence positive development of the immediate communities and to foster a cordial relationship with them.

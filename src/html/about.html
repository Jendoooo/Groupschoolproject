ABOUT US

BRIEF HISTORY OF OGUNMODEDE SENIOR COLLEGE

Ogunmodede Senior college , situated at 39,Ekundayo Street Oloja Estate, was founded in the year 1980. The schools objective approach to education led to instant recognition by the relevant authorities and stakeholders in education in Nigeria.

For over four decades, we have consistently turned out students who have gone on to lead different organsiations and government parastatals. Our curriculum is fit-for-purpose, coompetency-based and integrated and embedded within the current educational system and its exisiting schools but focuses on providing career-ready skills as an option for students who may have otherwise have pursued the pure academic part.

Over the years, our curriculum has evolved to accommodate programmes that meet national requirement. The school presently prepares students for examinations at various levels and the Senior School Certificate Examination such as WASSCE and NECO Exams. Equally ,we encourage our students to pick up 21st century skills and it is one of those encouragement that led to the creation of this website by the members of our programming club.

Our Mission

To produce highly diciplined students with sound,academic and extra curricular abilities capable of making them secure addmision into tertiary institution with less stress through the creation of beautiful and conducive environment and with capacity to influence environment and with capacity to influence positive development of the immediate communities and to foster a cordial relationship with them.

Achievement

Over the course of the past decades and especially in recent years, we have maintained giant strides in contributing our quota to the nation's educational system.

In 2020, our students had a 90% pass rate in the WASSCE examinations. We went a step further in 2021, securing a 92% pass rate in the same examinations ultimately contributing to Educational District III emerging as the district with the highest pass rate in the state and southwest region.

In 2022, our students outdid themselves , placing a state-high 93.8% pass rate in the examination. To lend credence to their extraordinary performance, nine of our students as underlisted were recipients of the BYINKS foundation scholarship sposnored by the former first lady of Lagos and wife of the current Minister of Works and Housing- Mrs Abimbola Fashola.
